## 💡 Quick Tips for Beginners

- **Start small**: Begin with a simple welcome email before complex campaigns
- **Quality over quantity**: Focus on engaged subscribers rather than list size
- **Test everything**: Always send test emails before launching campaigns
- **Mobile-first**: Over 50% of emails are opened on mobile devices
- **Be consistent**: Regular sending builds subscriber expectations

## 📊 Success Metrics for Beginners

Focus on these key metrics as you start:
- **Open Rate**: 15-25% is a good starting point
- **Click Rate**: 2-5% indicates engaging content
- **Unsubscribe Rate**: Keep below 0.5%
- **Bounce Rate**: Should be under 2%
