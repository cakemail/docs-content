# Choosing Your Plan

## Description

Choosing the right plan for your email marketing depends on a few key factors, such as the size of your contact list, the frequency of sending your campaigns, and the tools you need. Here's a practical guide to help you determine what best suits your needs.

## Why this is important

Selecting the appropriate email marketing plan is crucial for maximizing your return on investment while ensuring you have access to the features and capacity you need. An undersized plan can limit your growth and force you to upgrade mid-campaign, while an oversized plan wastes resources on unused features and capacity. The right plan choice directly impacts your ability to execute effective campaigns, maintain consistent communication with your audience, and scale your email marketing efforts as your business grows.

## What to look for in your plan choice

### Contact list size

Plans are based on the number of contacts you manage. It's important to select a plan that accommodates your current list size and leaves room for future growth.

### Sending limits

Each plan includes a specific sending limit. Evaluate the frequency of your campaigns to ensure the plan you choose matches your sending needs.

### Included features

Each plan comes with tools to help you create campaigns, but some plans include exclusive features, including:

- **Pop-up, landing pages and special forms**: those help you build your contact list and create promotions directly on your website go get more contacts! 
- **Behavioral segmentation:** Create more targeted campaigns by grouping your audience based on actions like opens and clicks, instead of just preferences
- **Drip automation:** Automate email sequences, such as onboarding series or promotional follow-ups, to save time and maintain engagement
- **A/B Testing:** Experiment with different subject lines, designs, or content to see what resonates best with your audience

## Accepted payment methods

Payment can be made with American Express, Visa, or Mastercard. Currently, we use Stripe (https://stripe.com), a US-based payment provider. Therefore, make sure your bank knows you're making international transactions if necessary.

## Troubleshooting

**Uncertain which plan to choose**

- Calculate your average monthly sends and contact growth rate
- Start with a smaller plan and upgrade as needed
- Contact support for personalized plan recommendations

**Hitting sending limits**

- Review your sending frequency and adjust campaign scheduling
- Consider upgrading to a plan with higher limits
- Segment campaigns to spread sends across multiple days

**International payment issues**

- Inform your bank about international transactions to Stripe
- Try a different credit card if payment fails
- Contact your bank to ensure international payments are enabled

**Unclear feature requirements**

- Test features with a free trial before committing
- Review the feature comparison chart on the pricing page
- Ask support for a demo of premium features

**Plan downgrade restrictions**

- Export important data before downgrading
- Ensure your contact list fits within lower plan limits
- Remove inactive contacts to reduce list size if necessary
