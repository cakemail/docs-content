# Managing your billing

## Description

![Interface screenshot](/images/billing/billing-managing-your-billing-395.png)


Our account options can be adapted according to your preferences and needs. This will guide you through the simple process of managing your account billing.

## Why this is important

![Interface screenshot](/images/billing/billing-managing-your-billing-396.png)


We offer different account options to meet your needs. With the free account, you'll be able to interact with your audience immediately, and with growth plans, you'll be able to continue developing your business. If your account exceeds the free plan, you must select one of our growth plans. Additionally, our premium plans are available for businesses that require more specialized tools.

## Subscription cancellation

![Click interface screenshot](/images/billing/billing-managing-your-billing-398.png)

In the **Billing** tab, you can close your account by clicking **Cancel subscription**. Then, you can start the account cancellation process. An automatic support request will be opened on your behalf to ensure the process goes smoothly. Our support team will contact you to confirm the changes and help you with the next steps.

## How contact deletions affect your billing cycle

When you delete contacts from your account, the change is **not applied immediately** to your billing.

The updated number of contacts will only take effect **at the start of your next billing cycle** (your next payment date).

Until that moment, your current plan continues to count the contacts that were present before deletion.

 Once the new billing period begins, your account is recalculated and the lower contact total is applied automatically.
