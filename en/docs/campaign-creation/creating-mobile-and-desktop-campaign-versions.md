# Creating Mobile and Desktop Campaign Versions

## Description

You can create two versions of your email – one for mobile and one for desktop – with different content for each. This can be useful if you want to optimize your email for different devices, or if you want to show or hide certain elements based on the device on which the email is viewed.

## Why It's Important

With more than 60% of emails now opened on mobile devices, creating device-specific versions of your campaigns is crucial for maximizing engagement and conversion rates. Mobile and desktop users have different reading patterns, screen sizes, and interaction behaviors that require tailored approaches. By optimizing content, layout, and call-to-action placement for each device type, you ensure that every subscriber has an optimal viewing experience.

## Step-by-Step Instructions
Here's a detailed guide on how to create a campaign for mobile and desktop:

<Stepper>

1. Create your campaign
   - Log into your account and click on the "Campaigns" tab in the top menu
   - Click on the "Create Campaign" button
   - You can choose a template for your campaign or start from scratch. We offer various templates optimized for mobile and desktop, so you can choose the one that best suits your needs.

1. Design your campaign
   - **Customize the design** of your campaign. The email designer can add and arrange elements, such as text, images, and buttons
   - You can also customize colors and fonts to match your brand.

1. Add content
   - **Add content** to your campaign. Use the editor to write your campaign text, add images, and insert links.

1. Configure device-specific display
   - **Select the element** you want to hide by clicking on it in the email designer
   - In the element settings panel on the right, look for the **"Hide on"** option
   - Click on the dropdown menu next to "Hide on" and select either **"Mobile"** or **"Desktop"** to hide the element on that device
   - **Repeat these steps** for any additional elements you want to hide or show on specific devices.

1. Preview and test
   - **Preview your campaign** to make sure it looks like what you want on mobile and desktop

1. Configure campaign settings
   - Configure your campaign settings, including recipients, send date, and subject line.

1. Launch your campaign
   - Review your campaign to make sure everything is correct, then click the "Send" button to launch your campaign

</Stepper>

There you have it! You've now created a campaign that will be optimized for mobile and desktop.

## Troubleshooting

**Elements don't hide on selected device**

- Make sure you've saved changes after setting "Hide on" options
- Clear cache and refresh preview
- Test using real devices rather than just browser preview

**Mobile version looks cramped**

- Use single-column layouts for mobile versions
- Increase font sizes for better mobile readability
- Reduce padding and margins for mobile elements

**Desktop elements appear on mobile**

- Double-check that "Hide on mobile" setting is enabled
- Some email clients may not support device-specific hiding
- Consider using responsive design as a fallback

**Preview doesn't match live email**

- Test with actual email sends to different devices
- Email client rendering may differ from preview
- Use email testing tools for accurate cross-client preview

**Content alignment issues**

- Center content for better mobile display
- Use percentage-based widths instead of fixed pixels
- Test with various screen sizes and orientations
