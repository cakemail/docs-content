# Creating Campaigns

## Description

Email marketing campaigns consist of an email sent simultaneously to multiple people with a specific objective; businesses worldwide use them to help them sell, connect, and achieve their goals. In the long term, you may strive to get your subscribers to sign up for a webinar or buy something on your site, but in the short term, you should primarily focus on reader interest and strengthening your relationship. Read more about this in our content strategy article.

## Why It's Important

The majority of people check their email multiple times a day, so it makes sense to send emails to your customers about your product. A direct relationship with your audience can only be established through this. Therefore, email marketing is the best way to nurture prospects. With just a few clicks, you can connect with hundreds, thousands, or even more customers.

Text, images, videos, animations, and much more can all be used to convey your message. Your results will be tracked, such as how many people opened your campaign, clicked on it, how many were marked as spam, etc. Ultimately, it's a proven cost-effective marketing strategy.

## Step-by-Step Instructions
You can send a campaign to your recipients with our email marketing application. You have several options available when creating a campaign; here's a brief overview of how to get started.

To begin the creation process, select the **Create** tab in the upper right corner of the application, and click on **Campaign**.

![Screenshot of the creation interface](/images/campaign-creation/4404612263707.jpeg)
You can then choose from the following options:

- Use your own templates - regular or already branded
- Use a professional template
- Reuse a previously sent campaign
- Design your own template

Once you've selected an option, you'll be directed to the email designer where you can customize and modify your template. Learn more about the email designer.

If you have **branded** your account by uploading your logo and including your color code, you'll have access to **Smart Templates**, a selection of templates created to automatically display your brand to make your email neat and professional.

## Testing and Sending Your Campaign
Whatever choice you made for your templates, it's very important to test your campaign thoroughly before sending emails to your customers, especially with a new design. Test it on different email browsers and don't forget to check on your phone too. Displaying a template on any platform can be complex! Put in a subject line, choose a time and date, sit back and watch the results!

You can use various subjects as content for your campaign. We suggest contacting us if you're still not sure what to use for your content, we'll be happy to discuss your business and help you get started. You can also read our various articles about this on our blog.

## Troubleshooting

**Email designer won't load**

- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try using Chrome or Firefox for better compatibility
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere

**Template won't save**

- Check your internet connection stability
- Save frequently when working on complex designs
- Use the auto-save feature if available

**Images not displaying**

- Make sure images are under 1MB for optimal loading
- Use supported formats (JPG, PNG, GIF)
- Upload images to the library before inserting them

**Campaign stays in draft**

- Check that all required fields are completed
- Verify that a recipient list is selected
- Make sure subject line and sender information are filled in

**HTML import issues**

- Validate your HTML code before importing
- Remove JavaScript and external CSS references
- Use inline CSS for better email client compatibility

**Preview doesn't match final email**

- Test sending to multiple email addresses
- Check rendering in actual email clients
- Account for email client limitations in design
