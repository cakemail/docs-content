# Deleting a Campaign Draft

## Description

It's possible to completely delete a campaign draft from your account. There is, however, a restriction on deleting campaigns; they can only be deleted in draft mode or before they are sent.

## Why It's Important

You'll be able to clean up your draft list using this option if it's cluttered with drafts you won't use and that cause confusion. You might have created a duplicate that no longer serves a purpose, so delete it here to keep your workspace organized.

**Important:** You cannot recover deleted drafts, so be careful not to delete something important.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

<Stepper>

1. Navigate to your Campaigns tab and locate the draft
![Campaign list showing campaign drafts](/images/campaign-creation/3D2ABF07-0266-46AA-B71A-D79E05D7A89C_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Select the **Delete** button next to the campaign name
![Location of Delete button in campaign row](/images/campaign-creation/61D0F67E-73C6-40A0-B8B7-9FC2B7C14E7C_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. The option to delete the draft will appear. Then, select **Delete Draft**
![Draft deletion confirmation dialog](/images/campaign-creation/96A692D8-6EEA-493C-A658-9E03053379E0_4_5005_c.jpeg)
</Stepper>


<Callout type="danger" title="Important Notice">
  Please note that once a draft is deleted, this action cannot be undone.
</Callout>

## Restrictions

- Campaigns can only be deleted in **draft mode**
- Once a campaign has been sent, it cannot be deleted
- This helps maintain campaign history and reporting accuracy

## Troubleshooting

**Delete button is not visible**

- Make sure the campaign is still in draft status
- Check that you have appropriate permissions to delete campaigns
- Refresh the page to update the campaign list

**Cannot delete sent campaign**

- Sent campaigns cannot be deleted for record keeping
- Archive old campaigns instead of deleting them
- Use filters to hide old campaigns from your main view

**Accidental deletion**

- Deleted drafts cannot be recovered
- Consider duplicating important drafts before major changes
- Save templates separately to preserve designs

**Bulk deletion issues**

- Delete campaigns one at a time for safety
- Clear browser cache if deletion seems stuck
- Contact support if multiple drafts won't delete
