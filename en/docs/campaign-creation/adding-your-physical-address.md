# Adding Your Physical Address

## Description

As part of our anti-spam policy, a physical address must be included in your email. Here are some guidelines on formatting the physical address found at the bottom of the email. There are two ways to do this:

- Add your physical address in the email designer
- Add your physical address in the HTML editor

## Why It's Important

Legally, this address must be provided for accountability purposes in order to identify the sender to the recipient. This can also help facilitate the unsubscribe process, prevent complaints, and help improve delivery.

## Legal Requirements

Please note that in the Commission's opinion, "mailing address" may be the sender's valid and current street (or civic) address, post office box address, rural route address, or general delivery address and must be valid for a minimum of **60 days after the message has been sent**.

## Step-by-Step Instructions
### Editing Your Physical Address in Your Profile

A physical address must be included to schedule a newsletter in order to protect subscribers (and yourself). If the system cannot detect the address merge field in the campaign, it will automatically add it to the bottom. Additionally, entering your business information is required when your account is created.

<Stepper>

1. Select Account from the menu in the top right
![Select Edit Email button](/images/campaign-creation/485F26F5-0D6B-4670-B865-2430AEAFD27E_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Select the Profile tab on the Account page
![Paste HTML code](/images/campaign-creation/FCAD0D54-BEF5-46E5-9058-B8E4A751B4AA_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. In the Profile options, click on the Pencil icon found to the right of Address (Required)
![Select Start with your own code](/images/campaign-creation/DDD01F59-144A-4FB6-A2BA-7DE791677C9B_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Update your address if necessary and click save.
</Stepper>







### Adding Your Physical Address in the Email Designer
You can add [CLIENTS.ADDRESS] wherever you want the address to appear in your campaign. Your business address will be pulled from the company profile page. It will then be possible to modify the [CLIENTS.ADDRESS] field with font, color, and size.

<Stepper>

1. Select Campaigns.
![Select campaign template](/images/campaign-creation/3D2ABF07-0266-46AA-B71A-D79E05D7A89C_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Select your campaign email template.
![Address update form](/images/campaign-creation/0E44E1D7-F230-4C01-8183-F606B426F1CF_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Select Edit Email.
![Merge tags usage menu](/images/campaign-creation/63842824-8F4D-4E01-83AA-021FD47A1891_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Type [CLIENTS.ADDRESS] where you want your physical address to appear
Customize it with text color, size, etc.
![Add CLIENTS.ADDRESS in HTML editor](/images/campaign-creation/A3405E2E-7DA4-4E95-B028-D9DF1B06417B_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Alternatively, you can add the physical address field with the Merge Tags option and by selecting Full Address.
![](/images/campaign-creation/Merge%20tags%20and%20clients%20address.gif)
You'll be able to see the result on the next page, where you can schedule the campaign.
</Stepper>

## Address Requirements

Your physical address must be:
- **Valid and current** for at least 60 days after sending
- **Complete** with street address or post office box
- **Accessible** for accountability purposes
- **Legally compliant** with anti-spam regulations

## Types of Acceptable Addresses

- Valid and current street (civic) address
- Post office box address  
- Rural route address
- General delivery address

## Benefits of Including Physical Address

- **Legal compliance**: Meets anti-spam law requirements
- **Trust building**: Shows transparency and legitimacy
- **Improved delivery**: Helps with email deliverability
- **Complaint prevention**: Provides clear sender identification
- **Professional appearance**: Demonstrates established business presence

## Best Practices

- Keep your address information updated in your profile
- Use consistent formatting across all campaigns
- Place address information in the email footer for visibility
- Ensure the address remains valid for the required period

## Troubleshooting

**Address doesn't appear in email**

- Check that the address merge field is properly inserted
- Verify your profile contains a complete physical address
- Preview the email to confirm automatic addition to bottom

**Campaign won't send**

- Ensure a valid physical address is included
- Update your profile address if missing or incomplete
- Verify the address meets legal requirements (not just email/web)

**Address formatting issues**

- Use standard address formatting for your country
- Avoid special characters that may not display correctly
- Test how the address appears in different email clients

**Profile address won't update**

- Save changes after updating your profile information
- Log out and log back in to refresh cached data
- Contact support if changes don't persist

**Compliance warnings**

- Verify the address will remain valid for 60+ days
- Don't use temporary addresses or virtual offices without physical presence
- Ensure PO box addresses are properly registered and active
