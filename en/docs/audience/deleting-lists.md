# Deleting Lists

## Description

Deleting a contact list is a simple process, but it's important to make sure you're ready to proceed, as this action cannot be undone. Follow the steps below to delete or empty a contact list:

## Why It's Important

Properly managing your contact lists, including knowing when and how to delete them, is crucial for maintaining an organized email marketing process. Deleting obsolete or unnecessary lists helps keep your account clean, improves navigation efficiency, and ensures you focus on active and relevant audiences. This practice also helps with compliance requirements, as maintaining outdated contact data can pose privacy risks.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

<Stepper>

1. Select your contact lists

1. Locate the specific list you want to delete or empty

1. Click on the **gear icon (⚙)** to access the list settings
![Screenshot of deletion interface](/images/manage-contacts/manage-contacts-how-to-delete-contact-lists-117.png)
</Stepper>

### Choose Your Action

Click on the arrow next to the settings menu to reveal two options:

#### **Delete list:** This will permanently delete the entire list and all associated contacts

#### **Empty entire list:** This will remove all contacts from the list but keep the list itself for future use

![Screenshot of deletion interface](/images/manage-contacts/manage-contacts-how-to-delete-contact-lists-118.png)

#### Confirm Your Decision
To finalize the deletion or emptying of the list, you will be prompted to type the word **"Delete"** as confirmation.
Once confirmed, the action will be completed.

![Screenshot of deletion interface](/images/manage-contacts/manage-contacts-how-to-delete-contact-lists-119.png)

## Key Points to Remember

**Irreversible action:** Deleting a list is permanent. Make sure you no longer need the list or its contacts before proceeding.

**Empty a list:** If you think you might need the list structure later, consider emptying it rather than deleting it entirely.

**Impact on attached items:** Deleting a list will also remove anything attached to it, including subscription forms, previously sent campaigns, and automations.

By following these steps, you can effectively manage your contact lists and maintain a clean and organized audience.

## Troubleshooting

**Unable to delete list - option grayed out**

- Check if the list has active automations
- Make sure all campaigns using the list are completed
- Verify you have administrator permissions
- Stop any ongoing imports to this list

**Deletion confirmation won't accept "Delete"**

- Type "Delete" exactly with a capital D
- Don't include quotes or extra spaces
- Make sure you're typing in the correct field
- Try copying and pasting the word

**List still appears after deletion**

- Refresh your browser page
- Log out and log back into your account
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Wait a few minutes for deletion to process

**Unable to empty list - contacts remain**

- Check if there are contacts in processing status
- Make sure no import is in progress
- Try deleting contacts in smaller batches
- Check that all segments are also cleared

**Error when deleting list with forms**

- Disconnect subscription forms first
- Remove the list from active automations
- Delete or reassign campaigns to another list
- Check for embedded forms on websites

**Want to recover a deleted list**

- Deletion is permanent - no recovery possible
- Check if you have a recent export backup
- Contact support immediately if deleted by mistake
