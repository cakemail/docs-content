# Removing a Contact

## Description

Contacts can choose to unsubscribe from your list, they can do so by clicking on an [UNSUBSCRIBE] link in an email campaign you sent them, or it can be done manually (a contact can send you an email with an unsubscribe request, for example). Learn how to add an unsubscribe link to your email. Similarly, you can permanently delete a user's information from your list. We'll explain how to do this in this article both ways.

**Two options are available, but the consequences are different**

- Unsubscribe a contact: you keep all the related information to it: emails received, opened and clicked.
- Permanently delete a contact: you loose all the related information to it.

## Why It's Important

If you handle manual unsubscribe requests properly, you can maintain a good sender reputation. Therefore, your deliverability won't be affected. 

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Unsubscribing a Contact

To manually unsubscribe a contact, follow these steps:

<Stepper>

1. Select the **Contacts** tab
![Select the Contacts tab](/images/audience/082ED574-5063-48A2-88B4-7AC675D1674A_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Choose your list
![Choose your list](/images/audience/CE3A5EE2-085F-4616-B867-C695822E58D2_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Find, using the magnifying glass, the contact's email
![Search for a contact](/images/audience/7FD16F10-C04E-46BA-8FB9-E1384E2C1AA2_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Click on the **...** in the contact's row
![Click on the three dots menu](/images/audience/A7006973-F052-4FBB-A9CD-195908E887AC_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Select **Unsubscribe**
![Select the Unsubscribe option](/images/audience/35383A02-3052-455E-A24E-3F840643410F_4_5005_c.jpeg)
*You can also edit, delete, or add this contact to the suppression list.*

1. The unsubscribe message will appear at the top of your page to confirm the action
</Stepper>

## Important Considerations

- **Unsubscribe vs Delete**: Unsubscribing maintains records for compliance, while deletion permanently removes all data
- **Compliance**: Always honor unsubscribe requests quickly to maintain good sender reputation
- **Data loss**: Permanent deletion cannot be undone - use with caution
- **Number of contacts update:** 
  When you delete contacts from your account, the change is **not applied immediately** to your billing. The updated number of contacts will only take effect **at the start of your next billing cycle** (your next payment date).

## Troubleshooting

**Unable to unsubscribe contact**

- Check if the contact is already unsubscribed
- Verify you have the proper permissions
- Make sure the contact exists in the selected list
- Try using manual unsubscribe option

**Unsubscribed contact still receiving emails**

- Check if the contact is on multiple lists
- Verify unsubscribe was applied to all lists
- Allow 24-48 hours for full propagation
- Check for active transactional emails

**Unable to permanently delete contact**

- Make sure the contact isn't part of an active automation
- Check if deletion is blocked by compliance rules
- Verify you have administrator permissions
- Try unsubscribing first, then deleting

**Deleted contact reappears**

- Check if the contact is being re-imported
- Verify deletion was confirmed correctly
- Look for duplicate entries with variations
- Review recent import logs

**Unsubscribe link not working for contact**

- Check that the link includes proper merge tags
- Verify the campaign was sent from the correct list
- Make sure the unsubscribe page is working
- Test with a different email client

**Contact requests to re-subscribe**

- Use the contact edit option to change status
- Make sure you have written consent
- Document the re-subscription request
- Check if the contact is on the suppression list
