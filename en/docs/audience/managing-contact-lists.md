# Managing Contact Lists

## Description

A contact list will contain the name and email address of each contact, as well as any other information you deem relevant. By upgrading to a Growth plan, you'll have access to creating new lists, we'll show you how. Find out how to upgrade your account.

## Why It's Important

By building a good and detailed contact list, you'll not only be able to distribute campaigns easily and securely, but you'll also be able to track the statistics they provide compared to your other lists.

## Duplicate Emails

Please note that multiple lists can have the same email address. It's important to note that a contact who is active in, for example, five lists will count as five contacts in your total contact count. We recommend using one list per language: this helps you keep all information about your contacts (history, campaigns received, clicks, etc.) in one place, so you don't have to pay for multiple rates. Segmentation within your list can help avoid this. Learn how to segment in our related article.

## Troubleshooting

**Unable to create a new list**

- Check that you have a Growth or Premium plan
- Verify your plan's list limit hasn't been reached
- Make sure you have administrator permissions
- Try using a different list name without special characters

**Default sender won't save**

- Check that the sender email is authenticated
- Verify the email domain is verified
- Make sure the sender exists in your sender list
- Try setting up the sender address first

**Duplicate contacts across lists**

- Use segmentation instead of multiple lists
- Consider consolidating lists by language
- Export and deduplicate before importing
- Review your contact management strategy

**List settings won't update**

- Click "Save" after each change
- Check for validation errors
- Make sure all required fields are filled
- Try updating one setting at a time

**Custom redirect pages not working**

- Check that URLs are complete (include https://)
- Verify pages are publicly accessible
- Test URLs in a private browser window
- Make sure URLs don't contain spaces or special characters

**Can't access list management options**

- Confirm you're clicking the correct gear icon
- Check your user permissions for list management
- Try logging out and logging back in
- Clear browser cache and cookies
