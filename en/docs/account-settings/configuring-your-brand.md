# Configuring Your Brand

## Description

This section features that allow you to customize your account's branding, ensuring that your confirmation pages reflect your brand's image. This guide walks you through the steps to manage your brand settings and maintain consistency across all form subscriptions pages and also displayed automatically in your Smart templates.

## Why it matters

Maintaining consistent branding across all marketing materials and platforms is essential for building brand recognition and establishing credibility.

## Step-by-step instructions

<Stepper>

1. Click on your company name/email address in the top right corner (this opens a dropdown menu), then click Brand

1. Upload your logo
![Logo upload interface](/images/accounts/28721468742043.png)

1. Set your logo properties
Once uploaded, click on the logo you want to set as **Primary**. Make sure your logo file is in the correct format and size before uploading. We recommend using a transparent PNG file with dimensions of 400 pixels wide by 200 pixels high.
![Définir le logo principal](/images/accounts/28721501348891.png)

1. Set the title color
Select the title color for all your confirmation pages. Click **Title Color**, then choose your preferred color from the palette.
![Select title color option](/images/accounts/15276600282267.png)
![Choose a color from the palette](/images/accounts/28721501351963.png)

1. Set the paragraph color
Select the paragraph color for all your confirmation pages. Click **Paragraph Color**, then choose your preferred color from the palette.
![Select paragraph color](/images/accounts/15276626279579.png)

1. Set the button text color
Set the button text color for your confirmation pages. Click **Button Text Color** and choose your preferred color from the palette.
![Select button text color](/images/accounts/15276676225435.png)

1. Set the button background color
Set the button background color using the **Button Background Color** option. Choose a color that complements your brand and enhances your brand image.
![Set background color](/images/accounts/15276678552347.png)

1. Set the background color
Set the background color for your confirmation pages using the **Background Color** option. Choose a color that reflects your brand and enhances the user experience.
![Preview and Publish buttons](/images/accounts/15276750859163.png)

1. Preview and publish
After setting all your brand settings, click **Preview** to see how your changes will appear. If you're satisfied with the changes, click **Publish** to save them and apply them to your account's confirmation pages.
![Preview and Publish buttons](/images/accounts/mceclip7.png)
</Stepper>
## Troubleshooting

**Logo upload fails**
- Ensure file size is under 2MB
- Use supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG
- Check image dimensions (recommended: 400x200px)
- Try converting the image to PNG format

**Colors don't display correctly**
- Clear your browser cache
- Click "Publish" to save changes
- Allow up to 5 minutes for changes to propagate
- Verify color contrast meets accessibility standards

**Preview doesn't show changes**
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Save changes before previewing
- Refresh the preview page
- Try a different browser

**Brand settings don't apply to confirmation pages**
- Verify you clicked "Publish" after making changes
- Check that you're editing the correct account
- Log out and log back in to refresh settings
- Contact support if changes don't appear after 24 hours

**Cannot access brand settings**

- Confirm you have administrator or brand manager permissions
- Check if this feature is available on your plan
- Ensure your account is in good standing
