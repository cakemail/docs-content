# Setting Your Timezone

## Description

To ensure your emails are scheduled according to your preferred time, you need to set your timezone accurately. This guide walks you through the steps to change your timezone.

## Why it matters

Timing is essential in email marketing. Make sure your emails are sent when you want them to be sent.

## Step-by-step instructions

Accessing your account settings

<Stepper>

1. Click on your company name/email address in the top right corner (this opens a dropdown menu), then click Users

1. Click on the user account whose timezone you want to change.

1. Adjust the timezone
On the user page, locate the timezone option. Click the dropdown menu and select your preferred timezone from the available options.

1. Save changes
After selecting the correct timezone, click **Save** to apply the changes.
![Sauvegarder les changements de fuseau horaire](/images/accounts/accounts-setting-up-your-timezone-133.png)
Note that changing your timezone only affects your account's scheduling time, not the recipient's timezone. Consider your target audience's timezone when scheduling email campaigns.
Setting your timezone accurately ensures your email campaigns are scheduled at your preferred time. By following these steps, you can adjust your timezone to match your location and schedule campaigns effectively. Remember to consider your target audience's timezone for optimal delivery timing.
</Stepper>

## Troubleshooting

**Timezone changes don't take effect**
- Save changes by clicking the "Save" button
- Log out and log back in
- Clear browser cache
- Check scheduled campaigns for updates

**Cannot find my timezone**
- Search for your nearest major city
- Use UTC offset if city isn't listed
- Check daylight saving time options
- Contact support for missing timezones

**Scheduled emails send at wrong time**
- Verify your timezone setting is correct
- Check daylight saving time adjustments
- Review campaign scheduling settings
- Consider recipients' timezones

**Multiple users with different timezones**
- Each user must set their own timezone
- Account-level timezone affects reporting
- Campaign scheduling uses sender's timezone
- Coordinate team schedules accordingly

**Timezone reverts to previous setting**
- Ensure you have permission to modify settings
- Check browser autofill interference
- Try using a different browser
- Contact support if issue persists
