# Adding and Editing Users

Learn how to add new users to your account and manage them effectively. This guide covers user account creation and best practices for team collaboration.

## Description

User accounts allow authorized team members to access your account. The system identifies users by their username, which is the email address used when signing up. This username serves as the login name. Users are team members who have been invited to share an account, and they can be assigned different permission levels.

## Why it matters

Managing users effectively helps you organize your team and maintain smooth workflows. Understanding how to use the user management page is crucial for creating new users and modifying existing ones.

## Step-by-step instructions

### To add new users to your account

<Stepper>

1. Hover over your account name and click **Users**
![Email invitation interface](/images/account-settings/986C7D39-B485-4428-B12C-9C34835D4F1D_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Select the **+ Add User** button to invite team members
![User editing interface](/images/account-settings/2F13BAF1-BDC1-4E38-BEEC-92CCB387F6E2_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Enter their information and click **Send invitation**
Each user can work in their own language.
![Add User button location](/images/account-settings/E798A18E-079F-470F-9538-63C0937CE080.png)

1. Users must accept the email invitation by clicking the link
![User information form](/images/account-settings/D131FD0F-057B-4226-AD45-9F526340A4C9.png)

1. Next, users must create a password for their account
![Access Users menu](/images/account-settings/FF847814-59C9-45F5-B034-ADF5F4B7937A.png)
Make sure your team knows how to create strong passwords to ensure your account is never compromised.
</Stepper>

### To edit users

<Stepper>

1. Hover over your account name and click **Users**
![Email invitation interface](/images/account-settings/986C7D39-B485-4428-B12C-9C34835D4F1D_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Click the **three dots "..."** on the right side of the user account

1. In the menu that appears, select **View**
![Password creation screen](/images/account-settings/367BDEEB-193B-44CD-9DA8-BBBF752D73FB_4_5005_c.jpeg)

1. Edit the user's information (note: email addresses cannot be changed)
![User management options menu](/images/account-settings/94D5C956-B526-4F06-ADE4-4540D33F0C5C.png)
</Stepper>


## Account sharing

We strongly recommend creating individual user accounts rather than sharing login credentials. Adding separate users for each team member prevents security risks and ensures proper access control.

## Troubleshooting

**Invitation email not received**

- Check spam/junk mail folder
- Verify the email address is correct
- Resend invitation after 24 hours
- Ensure your account hasn't reached its user limit

**"Permission denied" error when adding users**
- Confirm you have administrator privileges
- Check if your account plan allows additional users
- Contact your account administrator for access

**User cannot log in after accepting invitation**
- Ensure they created a password
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try using a different browser
- Reset password if necessary

**Cannot edit user information**
- Only administrators can modify user details
- Email addresses cannot be changed after creation
- To change an email, delete and recreate the user

**Duplicate user error**
- Email address is already registered
- Check active and pending invitations
- Delete existing user before re-adding
