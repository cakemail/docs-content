# Managing Senders

## Description

In this article, we'll discuss how to choose your sender name and email address while staying consistent with your brand, which will directly affect the delivery of your campaigns and your image. As a result, each message includes the sender and recipient addresses.

## Why it's important

The sending email server sees the "sender" domain when initiating the session and your recipients will see your "from" address. Using the same sender domain as the From domain improves delivery. Always remember, successfully delivering emails depends on one thing - sending emails to the right people consistently with your brand.

## Step-by-step instructions

Adding a verified sender—known as the "From" address—to your marketing campaigns is an important step. Here's how it's done:

<Stepper>

1. Click on your company name/email address in the top-right corner (this opens a dropdown menu), then click on Senders

1. Click on **+ Add sender**
After verification, any email address added to the sender list can be used. Please take a moment to read the following article before changing your sender email address: Changing my sender email address.
**Important reminder:** To ensure optimal deliverability and protect your sender reputation, it's crucial to send emails from an authenticated domain. For a detailed guide on setting up domain authentication, please refer to our domain authentication guide.
</Stepper>

## Troubleshooting

**Sender verification email not received**

- Check spam/junk folders for the verification email
- Ensure the email address is typed correctly without spaces
- Wait 10-15 minutes as emails may be delayed
- Request a new verification email from sender settings

**Unable to add sender email from free providers**

- Gmail, Yahoo, and other free email providers require domain authentication
- Use a professional domain email address instead
- If you must use a free provider, ensure your account has proper authentication
- Consider upgrading to a business email solution

**Sender email showing as "unverified"**

- Complete the email verification process by clicking the link in the verification email
- Check if the verification link has expired (usually 24-48 hours)
- Remove and re-add the sender email if verification continues to fail
- Contact support if email remains unverified after multiple attempts

**Recipients see a different sender name than configured**

- Clear your email client cache as it might display old sender information
- Verify the sender name doesn't contain special characters that might not display correctly
- Check if email clients are overriding display names with contact information
- Ensure you're using the correct sender profile for your campaign

**Unable to change default sender email address**

- Verify the new sender email address is already added and verified
- Check account permissions if you're using a team or reseller account
- Ensure no active campaigns are using the current default sender
- Try logging out and back in to refresh account settings
