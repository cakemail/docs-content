# Connecting Cakemail with Zapier

## Description

Zapier allows you to connect Cakemail with thousands of the most popular applications (no coding required), so you can automate your work and have more time for what matters most. Essentially, Zapier allows you to connect multiple software and tools together so they communicate and work together for you.

## Why This Matters

By automating information and processes with Zapier, you can reduce the time spent collecting statistics and data. This automation helps you quickly and easily access the information you need and make your email marketing efforts more successful without needing to code!

## Step-by-Step Instructions

<Stepper>

1. To get started, go to the **Cakemail integrations page**
![Navigate to the Cakemail integrations page](/images/integrations/Screen_Shot_2022-04-27_at_4.43.37_PM.png)

1. Once there, you can browse popular applications or search using the search bar
There are many to choose from including:
- Google Sheets and Google Docs
- Google Analytics
- Slack
- Shopify
- Facebook Lead Ads
- HubSpot
- …and many more.

1. Once you find the application(s) you want to connect, click on the name and icon
This will take you to a page explaining how to connect Cakemail with that application.
In this example, we chose Google Sheets.

1. Click on the orange button: **'Connect Cakemail + Google Sheets'**
![Connect Cakemail + Google Sheets button](/images/integrations/Screen_Shot_2022-04-27_at_4.43.22_PM.png)

1. From there, you'll need to set up your first trigger
A trigger is simply an action that must take place for another action to occur. For example, you can set up a trigger that adds a new contact in Cakemail to your Google Sheet.
![Select trigger event from dropdown menu](/images/integrations/Screen_Shot_2022-04-27_at_4.43.07_PM.png)
**Note:** You can also name the trigger, called a "Zap" at the top of this page.
You can also set up other trigger events. For example:
- Contact updated
- Contact unsubscribed
- New email open
- New email click

1. When you've made your choice, click **Continue**

1. Next, you'll need to copy the spreadsheet in Google Sheets and ensure your files are set up correctly
![Spreadsheet copy setup instructions](/images/integrations/Screen_Shot_2022-04-27_at_4.48.28_PM.png)

1. Choose the application and event
![Application and event selection setup](/images/integrations/Screen_Shot_2022-04-27_at_4.48.37_PM.png)

1. Choose the Google Sheets account in this submenu
![Select Google Sheets account](/images/integrations/Screen_Shot_2022-04-27_at_4.48.45_PM.png)

1. Set up the action by selecting your Google Drive, Spreadsheet, Worksheet, and Email
![Configure Google Sheets action settings](/images/integrations/Screen_Shot_2022-04-27_at_4.48.57_PM.png)

1. Click **Test & Continue** to verify and evaluate this action
![Test & Continue button](/images/integrations/Screen_Shot_2022-04-27_at_4.49.05_PM.png)

1. If the test was successful, you can then activate your newly created Zap! Click **Turn on Zap**
![Turn on Zap button](/images/integrations/Screen_Shot_2022-04-27_at_4.49.14_PM.png)
Now that your Zap has been created, you can share it with others!
![Share your completed Zap](/images/integrations/Screen_Shot_2022-04-27_at_4.49.26_PM.png)
</Stepper>

## Troubleshooting

**Zapier connection won't authenticate**

- Check that you're using the correct Cakemail API credentials
- Verify if your API key has the necessary permissions
- Ensure you're connected to the correct Cakemail account
- Try regenerating your API key and reconnecting

**Triggers don't fire when expected**

- Test the trigger manually using Zapier's test function
- Check that the trigger event actually occurs in Cakemail
- Check if there's a delay (Zapier checks can take 1-15 minutes)
- Ensure your Zapier plan supports the necessary polling frequency

**Data doesn't map correctly between applications**

- Review field mappings to ensure proper data types
- Check for required fields that might be empty
- Verify that date and time formats match between systems
- Test with sample data to identify mapping issues

**Zap suddenly stopped working**

- Check if API credentials have expired or changed
- Verify that both connected applications are still active
- Review Zapier task history for error messages
- Ensure you haven't exceeded your Zapier plan limits
