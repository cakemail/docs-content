# Creating Content with AI

## Description

Creating compelling and engaging content is fundamental to successful email marketing campaigns. Our application offers an innovative feature that simplifies content creation by generating paragraphs based on prompts, similar to the technology used in chatbots. This article will guide you through using the application's AI-powered paragraph generation feature, highlighting its benefits and demonstrating how it can enhance your email campaigns.

## Why This Matters

**Time efficiency**: 
The application's AI-powered feature saves time by automating content creation, eliminating the need to write paragraphs from scratch.

**Consistency**:
Generated paragraphs maintain consistent tone and style, ensuring cohesive messaging throughout your email campaigns.

**Inspiration and ideas**:
Generated content can serve as a starting point, providing inspiration and ideas for customizations and improvements.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

<Stepper>

1. Log in to your account and create a campaign 

1. Design your email content 

1. Add a paragraph block to your email design 
![AI assistant to generate paragraph content 267](/images/campaign-creation/campaigns-using-the-ai-assistant-to-generate-paragraph-content-267.png)

1. Select your paragraph block 
Click on **Write with AI** on the right side of the screen
![Screenshot of creation interface](/images/campaign-creation/campaigns-using-the-ai-assistant-to-generate-paragraph-content-268.png)

1. Enter prompts or topic keywords 
Describe the content you want the generated paragraphs to focus on
Examples: "Introduce our new product line" or "Highlight the benefits of our service"

1. Click Generate 
![Interface element using AI assistant to generate paragraph content](/images/ai/generate-button.png)

1. Review the generated paragraphs 
Choose the most appropriate option

1. Customize and refine the content 
Align with your specific messaging and branding
</Stepper>

## Best Practices

- **Use specific prompts** for better targeted content generation
- **Always review and customize** AI-generated content to match your brand voice
- **Use as a starting point** rather than final content
- **Test different prompts** to get varied content options
- **Combine AI assistance with human creativity** for optimal results

## Benefits for Email Marketing

- **Faster content creation** for regular campaigns
- **Consistent messaging** across multiple emails
- **Overcome writer's block** with fresh ideas
- **Maintain quality** even with frequent sends
- **Scale content production** effectively

## Troubleshooting

**The "Write with AI" button doesn't appear**

- Make sure you have selected a paragraph block in your email
- Check that your account plan includes AI features
- Verify you're using a supported email template
- Refresh the email designer and try again

**AI generates irrelevant or poor quality content**

- Provide more specific and detailed prompts
- Include context about your audience and tone
- Try different prompt variations to get better results
- Use AI content as a starting point and edit as needed

**Generated content doesn't match brand voice**

- Include brand tone instructions in your prompt
- Specify the style (formal, casual, professional, etc.)
- Edit the generated content to align with your brand
- Save successful prompts for future use
