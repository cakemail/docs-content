# Subscription redirection page

## Description

In this article, we'll show you how to redirect your subscribers to the landing page of your choice after they:
- Sign up
- Confirm their subscription  
- Unsubscribe

## Why It's Important

Your contacts can receive a more personalized experience when they subscribe and unsubscribe from your list. You can add your own logo to the landing page, making it more elegant, clearer, and aligned with your brand.

## Important Notes

- **New lists**: This feature is automatically deployed for new lists

- **Custom branding**: You can also use your own branded subscription, subscription confirmation, and unsubscription pages simply by modifying the URL

- **Logo requirement**: Make sure you've uploaded a logo in your company profile for optimal branding

## Types of Redirection Pages

You can customize redirection pages for:
- **Subscription confirmation**: After a person signs up
- **Double opt-in confirmation**: After a person confirms their subscription
- **Unsubscription confirmation**: After a person unsubscribes

## Benefits

- **Brand consistency**: Maintain your brand experience throughout the subscription process
- **Professional appearance**: Custom pages look more professional than default system pages
- **Better user experience**: Subscribers see familiar branding and messaging
- **Trust building**: Consistent branding builds trust with your audience

## Troubleshooting

**Logo doesn't appear on redirection pages**

- Make sure you've uploaded a logo in your company profile settings
- Check the logo file format (JPG, PNG recommended)
- Verify the logo file size isn't too large (under 500KB)
- Clear your browser cache and reload the page

**Custom URL doesn't redirect correctly**

- Check that the URL includes the protocol https:// or http://
- Verify the destination page is online and accessible
- Make sure there are no typos in the URL
- Test the URL directly in a browser before saving

**Changes don't reflect on redirection pages**

- Allow a few minutes for changes to propagate
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try viewing in an incognito/private browsing window
- Verify you clicked Save after making changes

**Different pages show for different actions**

- Check that you've set the correct URL for each action type
- Verify subscription settings vs unsubscription settings separately
- Make sure double opt-in settings match your intended flow
- Test each scenario (subscribe, confirm, unsubscribe) individually
