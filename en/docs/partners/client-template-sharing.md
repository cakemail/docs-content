# Client Template Sharing

## Description

The asset sharing feature allows you, as a reseller, to create, design, and share email templates with your users seamlessly. This guide will explain how to use this feature effectively to manage and distribute your templates.

## Why This Matters

By centrally managing and distributing templates, you ensure that your clients have access to high-quality designs that align with best practices, saving them time and improving their campaign effectiveness. This feature also allows you to maintain control over brand standards, offer value-added services to your clients, and differentiate your reseller offering in the competitive email marketing landscape.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Accessing the Assets Section

<Stepper>

1. Sign in to your reseller account.

1. Navigate to the "Assets" section
It's located in the top right of the dashboard, next to the "Accounts" tab.
![Template details form](/images/partners/bde8b9e2-9e95-4660-842c-d7f80e9dd341.png)

1. You'll see an organized list of your templates
   The categories are:
   - Free templates
   
   - Paid templates
   
   - Smart templates
   
     </Stepper>

### You can sort your assets
- All templates
- My templates
- General templates: This refers to your system email templates.
![Sharing options interface](/images/partners/6d7f826f-1ec3-4ab6-b832-2bb26f343629.png)

## Creating a New Template

#### Click the "+" button to initiate creating a new template.

![Auto-sharing toggle option](/images/partners/a313f9ee-138a-4b73-b89b-52114e9d7c84.png)

#### Enter the template details:
- **Name**: The title of your template, which should be descriptive and specific.
- **Subject**: This is the default subject line that will appear when the template is used in a campaign.
- **Category**: Choose whether the template will be listed as "Free" or "Paid".
- **Tags**: Add relevant tags to help categorize and make the template easily searchable (e.g., industry-specific tags, seasonal tags).

![](/images/partners/d03a7259-933d-4a88-8729-0330ddfae54f.png)

## Designing Your Template

Once your campaign is created, you'll be directed to the [Email Designer](/en/docs/campaign-creation/using-the-email-designer), where you can customize it with various content blocks:
- **Content blocks**: Drag and drop elements such as text areas, images, buttons, and tables to build your template.

![Template editor with content blocks](/images/partners/partners-sharing-email-templates-with-your-end-users-a-step-by-step-guide-178.png)

- **Customization**: Modify the layout, colors, and typography to align with your brand's aesthetic.

By using [smart templates](/en/docs/email-templates/working-with-smart-templates), you can ensure a consistent and professional look for all your sub-accounts' email templates.

- **Advanced settings**: Use advanced features like conditional content or dynamic personalization to enhance engagement.

### Save and Preview Your Template

<Stepper>

1. Preview the template by clicking the eye icon in the top right.
Ensure it displays correctly on both desktop and mobile formats.
![](/images/partners/10e0208b-92c5-4d2c-b926-699da9e7f860.png)

1. After finalizing your design, click "Save and Exit".
</Stepper>

## Sharing Your Template

After previewing, you can decide how to share the template:

![](/images/partners/ebeafa6d-06ed-4231-819e-69a793ff2046.png)

#### Choose who you want to share with
- With all your users: Click "Share with All"
- With specific users: Manually select the users to share with.

#### Unshare your template
Alternatively, you can also choose to unshare your template, either by clicking "Unshare with All" or by manually selecting which users to unshare with.

#### Enable auto-sharing
Auto-sharing: This allows you to automatically share your template with new accounts.

![](/images/partners/bf5c7f0d-3497-4765-9e62-7f8092d72329.png)

#### Click "Publish" once your sharing preferences are set.
![](/images/partners/57adb884-18b7-4deb-bd91-c1eba9a8e70c.png)

## How End Users Access Your Templates

End users will be able to access shared templates in their own accounts. Here's how they can find them:

#### Sign in to their account.
#### Click "Create Campaign."
#### Select "Change Template."
#### Navigate to the "[Partner Name] Templates" section.
#### Select the template and start customizing!

## Troubleshooting

**Templates don't appear for end users**

- Check that the template was properly published after sharing
- Verify if sharing settings include the specific users
- Ensure end users are looking in the correct template section
- Allow a few minutes for shared templates to propagate

**Can't edit or delete shared templates**

- Confirm you have the necessary reseller permissions
- Check if the template is currently being used by clients
- Verify you're logged into the correct reseller account
- Try unsharing the template before attempting to delete

**Auto-sharing not working for new accounts**

- Ensure auto-sharing is enabled in the template settings
- Check that new accounts are properly linked to your reseller account
- Verify if there are template sharing limits on your plan
- Review if specific template categories have sharing restrictions

**Template formatting issues after sharing**

- Test templates in preview mode before sharing
- Check that all images are hosted on accessible servers
- Verify if custom fonts are web-compatible or properly embedded
- Ensure responsive design works across different email clients
