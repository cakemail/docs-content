# Managing Client Features

## Description

As a reseller, you can manage your clients' access to advanced next-generation features from your reseller interface. This article will guide you through each step to manage these next-generation features available to sub-clients from the Cakemail reseller interface. Additionally, we'll provide an overview of each feature and its benefits.

## Why This Matters

Managing your clients' access to these features is crucial to ensuring their success. With the reseller interface provided by Cakemail, you can easily manage your clients' access to certain functions. These features, such as campaign templates, automation conditions, A/B split campaigns, and behavioral segmentation, allow your clients to create more personalized and targeted email campaigns that increase engagement and conversions.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

<Stepper>

1. Access the Cakemail reseller interface by logging into your Cakemail reseller account.

1. Click on the Clients tab to see a list of all your clients.
![Click on the Clients tab to see client list](/images/partners/partners-managing-your-clients-next-gen-features-164.png)

1. Hover over the client whose next-generation features you want to adjust to open the client submenu.

1. Select the Edit Account Limits option on the right side of the client dashboard.
![Select Edit Account Limits from client submenu](/images/partners/partners-managing-your-clients-next-gen-features-165.png)

1. Locate the Next Generation Features option and toggle it on or off to manage next-generation features for your clients.
![Toggle next-generation features options](/images/partners/partners-managing-your-clients-next-gen-features-166.png)

1. Click the Save button to save your settings after making your changes.
![Click Save button to save changes](/images/partners/partners-managing-your-clients-next-gen-features-167.png)
</Stepper>

## Next Generation Features Details

<Stepper>

1. Campaign Templates Enabled
Activates premium professional templates designed to help clients stand out in their email campaigns.

1. Automation Conditions Enabled
Activates conditional automation, such as if/then conditions, to create more personalized and targeted email campaigns.
Increases email campaign efficiency by automating specific tasks, such as sending follow-up emails to subscribers.

1. A/B Split Enabled
Activates A/B split campaigns to test different email content or designs and identify the most effective approach.
Provides insights on which campaign elements resonate best with subscribers and can be used to improve future campaigns.

1. Smart Email Resource Enabled
Not yet available in the UI, it will allow the use of "AI" features to optimize email campaigns.

1. Smart Template Enabled
Not yet implemented, but will be used for smart and customizable templates.

1. Brand Access Enabled
Not yet in the UI, but will allow user branding.
Enables businesses to maintain brand consistency across all email marketing efforts.

1. Custom Automation Webhooks Enabled
An API feature that allows custom webhooks to trigger specific actions in email campaigns.

1. HTML Editor Enabled
Activates the use of the HTML editor to provide more customization and design options.

1. Contact Export Enabled
Activates the use of the export feature to extract contact lists from email campaigns.

1. List Redirection Enabled
Activates the use of custom list redirection functionality to redirect subscribers to a specific landing page or website.

1. Custom Merge Tag Enabled
Activates the use of custom contact merge tags in email campaigns.
Personalizes email content and increases engagement by addressing subscribers by their name or other personal details.

1. Insert Reseller Logo
Injects the reseller badge into the footer of campaigns, redirect pages, and preference manager.
Increases brand recognition and promotes reseller services.

1. Behavioral Segmentation Enabled
This feature activates advanced subscriber segmentation based on behavior via the segment editor.
Increases email content relevance and improves engagement by targeting specific subscriber groups.

1. Tag Usage in Automation Enabled
Not yet in the user interface, but will allow the use of tags in automation.

1. Tag Usage Enabled
Activates the use of tags in email campaigns to segment subscribers based on preferences or behavior.
Provides more personalized and targeted email content that increases engagement and conversions.
</Stepper>

## Troubleshooting

**Client can't see enabled features**

- Check that features were saved after enabling them
- Verify if the client needs to log out and log back in
- Ensure the client's plan supports the enabled features
- Allow up to 15 minutes for changes to propagate

**Feature toggles are grayed out**

- Confirm you have reseller administrator permissions
- Check if your reseller plan includes these features
- Verify that the specific client account is active
- Contact support if features should be available

**Changes don't save properly**

- Ensure you clicked the Save button after making changes
- Check if there are error messages on the page
- Verify your internet connection is stable
- Try using a different browser if issues persist

**Client has exceeded feature limits**

- Review the client's current usage against their limits
- Check if features need to be upgraded in their plan
- Consider adjusting limits or upgrading the client's package
- Monitor usage regularly to prevent future issues
