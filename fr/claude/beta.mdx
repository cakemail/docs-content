### Safety Recommendations

To minimize risks during beta:

1. **Test First**: Always test with small lists or test campaigns
2. **Be Specific**: Use clear, specific commands to reduce misinterpretation
3. **Verify Before Sending**: Review campaigns before giving the send command
4. **Use Safeguards**: Set up approval workflows when possible
5. **Monitor Activity**: Regularly check your account activity
6. **Backup Data**: Keep backups of important contact lists

### Command Best Practices

To ensure the AI understands your intent:

#### ✅ Good Commands (Specific)

- "Create a draft campaign called 'March Newsletter' for my VIP list"
- "Show me the open rate for campaign ID 12345"
- "Add john@example.com to the Newsletter list"

#### ⚠️ Risky Commands (Vague)

- "Clean up my lists" (could delete data)
- "Send that campaign" (which campaign?)
- "Update everything" (too broad)
