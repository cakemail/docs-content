# Installation

### Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:
- [Claude Desktop](https://claude.ai/download) installed on your computer
- A [Cakemail account](https://www.cakemail.com/) (free account available)
- The <a href="/cakemail-extension-for-claude.dxt" download>Cakemail Extension for Claude</a>

## Installation Guide

### Step 1: Install the Extension

1. Open Claude Desktop
2. Navigate to Extensions (⚙️ Settings → Extensions)
3. Drag the downloaded cakemail-extension-for-claude.dxt file on the Claud Extensions window.
4. Click "Install" and wait for confirmation

### Step 2: Configure Your Account

1. After installation, you'll see a configuration prompt
2. Enter your Cakemail credentials:
   - **Email**: Your Cakemail account email
   - **Password**: Your Cakemail password
3. Click "Connect" to authenticate

### Step 3: Verify Connection

Test your connection by asking Claude:
```
"Check my Cakemail connection status"
```

You should see a success message with your account details.
