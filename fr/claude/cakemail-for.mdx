# Cakemail for Claude (beta)

## Getting Started

### Welcome to Cakemail Claude Extension

The Cakemail Claude Extension transforms how you manage email marketing by bringing the full power of Cakemail's platform into natural conversation with Claude. This documentation will help you get started and master all available features.

### Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:
- [Claude Desktop](https://claude.ai/download) installed on your computer
- A [Cakemail account](https://www.cakemail.com/) (free account available)
- The <a href="/cakemail-extension-for-claude.dxt" download>Cakemail Extension for Claude</a>

## Installation Guide

### Step 1: Install the Extension

1. Open Claude Desktop
2. Navigate to Extensions (⚙️ Settings → Extensions)
3. Drag the downloaded cakemail-extension-for-claude.dxt file on the Claud Extensions window.
4. Click "Install" and wait for confirmation

### Step 2: Configure Your Account

1. After installation, you'll see a configuration prompt
2. Enter your Cakemail credentials:
   - **Email**: Your Cakemail account email
   - **Password**: Your Cakemail password
3. Click "Connect" to authenticate

### Step 3: Verify Connection

Test your connection by asking Claude:
```
"Check my Cakemail connection status"
```

You should see a success message with your account details.

## Best Practices

### Campaign Creation

1. **Clear Naming Conventions**
   - Use descriptive names: "2025-03 March Newsletter" not "Newsletter"
   - Include dates for time-sensitive campaigns

2. **Subject Line Optimization**
   - Keep under 50 characters
   - A/B test different versions
   - Avoid spam trigger words

3. **Content Best Practices**
   - Mobile-optimize all designs
   - Include alt text for images
   - Balance text and images

### List Management

1. **Regular Cleaning**
   ```
   "Remove bounced emails from all lists"
   "Delete subscribers inactive for 12+ months"
   ```

2. **Segmentation Strategy**
   - Create segments based on engagement
   - Use purchase history for targeting
   - Test different segment criteria

### Performance Optimization

1. **Testing**
   ```
   "Send a test email to our team"
   "Create an A/B test for subject lines"
   ```

2. **Timing**
   - Test different send times
   - Consider timezone differences
   - Avoid major holidays

### Glossary
